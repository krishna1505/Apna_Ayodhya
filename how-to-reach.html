How to Reach Ayodhya ??

भगवान राम की भूमि पर आपका स्वागत है

By Bus

Ayodhya is well connected by road and several state-run and private buses operate routes to Ayodhya from major cities.

Key Bus Routes: Delhi-Ayodhya, Lucknow-Ayodhya, Varanasi-Ayodhya.

By Train

Ayodhya has a railway station that is connected to major cities. Trains to Ayodhya are available from Lucknow, Delhi, and Varanasi.

Key Trains: Ayodhya Express, Faizabad Junction.

By Plane

The nearest airport to Ayodhya is in Lucknow. You can fly into Lucknow and then take a taxi or bus to Ayodhya.

Airport: Chaudhary Charan Singh International Airport, Lucknow.
