Best Time to Visit

Ayodhya is a city with distinct seasons. The best time to visit is during the winter months from October to March when the weather is pleasant and ideal for exploring the city's historic and spiritual landmarks.

Monsoon season, from July to September, can be quite rainy and might disrupt travel plans. Summer, from April to June, can be extremely hot, making sightseeing uncomfortable.
